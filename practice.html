    Ingredients!

  1. 200g pasta

  2. 2 cloves garlic

  3. 1 cup tomato sauce

  4. 1 tbsp olive oil

  5. Salt

  6. Pepper

  7. Grated cheese optional

    Recipe!

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water

  2. Heat olive oil in a pan

  3. Cook garlic for 1–2 minutes

  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper

  5. Drain pasta and mix into sauce

  6. Serve with cheese if wanted
